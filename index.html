Writers's Block

Writers Block is a speculative writing website for idea generation and for writers of similar minds to congregate. Make an account to begin, and generate a prompt. After the generation of a prompt, one person in your group will be selected to start writing. When they hit a predetermined word count, the second person in the chain will read the most recent content, and then write their section of the story.
This will continue until either a prechosen word count is hit, or until all members vote to end the chain.
Afterwards the entire story will be open for all to read.
Either start your own story, or make one with friends, or even join a trending one anonymously, your worlds are at your fingertips.
